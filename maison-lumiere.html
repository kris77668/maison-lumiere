◆ Our Story

A century &
three generations of savoir-faire

From a single atelier on Rue de la Paix to the world's most discerning collectors, Maison Lumière has spent over 130 years pursuing one ideal: jewellery that transcends its time.

Every stone passes through twelve hands before it reaches the showroom. Every setting is the work of artisans whose apprenticeships are measured in decades, not months. We do not sell online — we receive, by appointment, those who wish to see.
